Yixin Liu

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Politics & International Affairs at Northern Arizona University. Prior to NAU, I was a postdoctoral fellow at the Maxwell School of Citizenship and Public Affairs, Syracuse University. I earned my PhD in 2022 from Florida State University. My research investigates cross-sectoral collaborative governance in the implementation of environmental policies. I am the recipient of the American Political Science Association’s Paul Volcker Junior Scholar Research Award for 2024.
