I am an incoming Post-doctoral fellow in Maxwell School of Citizenship and Public Affairs at Syracuse University. I received my PhD in 2022 at Florida State University. My research explores questions related to collaborative behaviors and outcomes in the field of environmental management. I am also a methodology enthusiast, my work utilizes causal methods in both experimental and observational studies.
